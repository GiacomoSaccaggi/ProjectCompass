<p>
    ProjectCompass is an intelligent aggregation and analysis platform designed to bring clarity and order to your software projects. It systematically gathers, organizes, and analyzes codebase data, providing a comprehensive overview that traditional tools often miss.
</p>

<h2>Key Features:</h2>

<ul>
    <li>
        <strong>Holistic Codebase Aggregation:</strong> ProjectCompass consolidates analysis and project data from across your entire codebase, bringing disparate information into one cohesive view.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Intelligent Ordering & Structuring:</strong> Say goodbye to information overload. Our smart algorithms process and logically arrange all collected data, making it easy to navigate and understand complex project landscapes.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Comprehensive Link Discovery:</strong> Beyond static analysis, ProjectCompass actively seeks out and associates all relevant links – from documentation and external resources to dependency mappings – directly with your codebase elements.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Actionable Insight Extraction:</strong> Unlock the true potential of your data. ProjectCompass doesn't just present information; it extracts meaningful insights, highlighting critical patterns, potential issues, and optimization opportunities to guide your development decisions.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong>Enhanced Project Visibility:</strong> Gain unparalleled visibility into your project's health, dependencies, and evolutionary path, empowering teams to make informed decisions and maintain a clear direction.
    </li>
</ul>
